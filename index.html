About Us

TAMAM GLOBAL CONSULT is a leading educational consultancy firm specializing in guiding students through the process of pursuing higher education abroad. With a focus on excellence and a deep understanding of international education, we provide comprehensive support from university selection to application.Our key services include assisting students in choosing suitable universities and programs based on their academic background and career aspirations. Through partnerships with a diverse network of universities worldwide, we ensure students have ample options to consider, taking into account factors like rankings, accreditation, and facilities.We are pleased to offer a proposal securing admissions and 50% scholarships for prospective students at specified universities. We negotiate substantial scholarship slots sponsored by governments to support students' academic pursuits.Additionally, we arrange affordable and student-friendly accommodation upon students' arrival, fostering a conducive learning environment. We also handle flight tickets and visa applications, ensuring a smooth journey from application to arrival at their chosen destination. Moreover, we provide vacation and tour packages tailored to individual preferences, making travel arrangements convenient and enjoyable.
